Executive Summary

This report analyses the efficiency and growth trends of major renewable energy in 2025.Solar energy leads in projected growth,while geothermal demonstrates the highest efficiency rate.Wind energy maintains steady expansion supported by global infrastructural investments.

Comparison Data

in 2026, solar energy leads renewable growth with 1450 GW installed capacity and a 22% annual increase.Wind follows with 1100 GW and 18% growth.Hydropower remains stable at 1300GW,while geothermal continues steady expansion at 95GW.
